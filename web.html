Connect hidemail to iCloud

This runs entirely on your machine. Nothing is sent to Apple or anyone else — the copied header goes straight back to the CLI on localhost.

  1. Sign in at icloud.com in this browser (Chrome or Firefox). Keep that tab open.
  2. Open Developer Tools: press F12 (or Cmd+Opt+I on Mac), and click the Network tab.
  3. Reload the iCloud tab. In the Network list, click any request whose name ends in icloud.com.
  4. Scroll to Request Headers, find Cookie, and copy its entire value (it's long).
  5. Paste it below and click Connect.

Tip (Chrome/Edge): right-click the request → Copy → Copy as cURL also works — paste the whole thing, we'll pull the cookie out.
